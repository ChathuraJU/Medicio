ABOUT US

Medi-Tenders is Sri Lanka’s only ONLINE marketplace that connects National Hospital Kandy and suppliers with ease. Currently, Medi-Tenders connect National Hospital Kandy only with this portal and our goal is to bring all 1118 curative healthcare institutions in Sri Lanka onto one online platform and provide visibility to SME’s and provide them with an online system to receive Tender Notices.
